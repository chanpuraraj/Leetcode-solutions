Problem 151 - Reverse Words in a string

Given a string s, reverse the order of its words. A word is any sequence of non-space characters, and words are separated by one or more spaces. The result should contain the words in reverse order, joined by a single space, with no leading, trailing, or extra spaces.

Example 1:
Input: "the sky is blue"
Output: "blue is sky the"

Example 2:
Input: " hello world "
Output: "world hello"
Explanation: Remove extra spaces at the start and end.

Example 3:
Input: "a good example"
Output: "example good a"
Explanation: Collapse multiple spaces between words to a single space.
